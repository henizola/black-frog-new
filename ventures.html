Scope of Our Work in Ethiopia

At Black Frog, we aim to open doors to a country that is full of potential. With us, you not only get to see the best of the country, but also invest in the possibility of a prosperous future. The company is a manifestation of the vision we have for Ethiopia. Ethiopia being the land where humankind began, and ancient civilizations flourished, is not getting the attention it deserves. Our purpose is to change that.

Subsidiaries & Categories
At our company, we specialize in three key areas: sales and marketing, technology, and advisory services.

Sales and marketing expertise

Exclusive Collaboration with Noah Real Estate: For the past 10 years, we have been dedicated partners with Noah Real Estate, specializing in crafting and executing targeted sales and marketing strategies tailored specifically for the real estate industry. Our extensive experience in this sector has allowed us to develop a nuanced understanding of market dynamics and customer preferences.

Red Brick (Tour and Travel Business)

Unique Travel Experiences: Red Brick is our tour and travel division, offering a curated selection of unique travel experiences and destinations. Whether it's corporate retreats, team-building trips, or executive travel, Red Brick delivers exceptional service and memorable journeys.

Roomroming (Hotel Accommodation Services for Businesses)

Tailored Accommodation Solutions: Roomroming is our specialized service catering to businesses' hotel accommodation needs. We excel at providing customized solutions for corporate travel, ensuring convenient bookings, negotiated rates, and seamless arrangements, saving businesses time and resources.

Call Center Management / Telemarketing (Enterprise Solutions)

Enhancing Customer Engagement: Our call center management and telemarketing services are designed to optimize customer engagement for enterprises. We leverage cutting-edge technology and industry best practices to deliver effective communication solutions, driving customer satisfaction and business growth.

Advisory Services (Real Estate Consulting and Research)

Consultative Approach: In our advisory role, we act as trusted consultants specializing in real estate. We conduct in-depth research, analyze market trends, and provide strategic insights to empower our clients to make informed decisions. Our advisory services encompass a range of real estate topics, from investment analysis to market feasibility studies.
Each area represents our commitment to excellence and innovation, backed by years of experience and a passion for delivering tangible results to our clients.
